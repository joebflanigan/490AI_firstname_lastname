AI Class Project

Artificial Intelligence in Economics | Fall 2025

Project Overview

This project examines how AI and machine learning can support managerial decision-making in economics. I built a small pipeline for data cleaning, feature engineering, and model training to forecast a business-relevant outcome. The write-up discusses modeling choices, evaluation metrics, and limitations, including fairness and interpretability concerns. Results are framed in terms of managerial insights and next steps for deployment.
